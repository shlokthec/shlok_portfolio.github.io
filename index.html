About Me

Fullstack Developer

Hey there! I'm Shlok, a 16-year-old tech enthusiast with a passion for programming and all things AI. As a 10th grader, I have already dipped my toes into Python, C, and C++ programming languages, pushing the boundaries of what I can create. My curiosity has led me to work with cutting-edge technologies such as OpenAI's GPT-API, which I used to build an impressive AI project, and Google Speech APIs to develop a voice-activated AI assistant that simplifies daily tasks. I'm always eager to learn more and explore new horizons. Currently, I'm delving into the world of embedded programming and robotics, working on an exciting project that involves building a toy robot capable of following voice commands like "forward" and "backward." My ultimate goal is to make a positive impact on the world through technology and inspire others to do the same.
